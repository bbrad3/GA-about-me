My Background

In 2018 I graduated from Colorado State University with a B.A. in Geology and have worked as an Engineering Tech and Environmental Tech. Though my professional experience I have learned that the career path I was on would not allow me to acheive my professional or personal. I am not one to settle, so I when I found coding I dove in head first!

My Experience

After losing my job due to the pandemic I moved home and had knee surgery. While sitting on the couch I decided to use my time wisely by learning to code. I began on the fantastic site freecodecamp.org where I learned the basics of HTML and CSS. I was hooked! I then took a Udemy course by Colt Steele, The Web Developer Bootcamp. In this course I was able to dive deeper into JavaScript, Bootstrap, and back-end programming in Node.js with Express and MongoDB. In addition to these recourses I dove deeper into JS by reading Head First JavaScript Programming A Brain-Friendly Guide by Eric Freeman and Elisabeth Robson. What a fantastic resource!
